Chilli-Topped Potato Soup

Description:

Savor a satisfying chili-smothered potato that not only fills you up but also helps you meet your macro gaols for lean bulking.

Ingredients:

  • 3 pounds baking potatoes
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
